Profile | SWELL



Last update: 17 April, 2020


An open academic seminar series for everyone interested in water economics.

To receive information about our seminars, including Zoom URLs, sign up by registering through this form.

n case you are willing to present at a SWELL seminar, please contact Erik Ansink. Proposals for presentations will be accepted on a rolling basis and are considered by a small editorial team.

Presentations follow a standard academic 30-minute format + 15 minutes moderated discussion. It takes place on Zoom, Tuesdays at 11:30AM eastern time which corresponds to 5:30PM CEST (exceptions indicated in the schedule).
