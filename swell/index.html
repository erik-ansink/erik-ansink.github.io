Profile | SWELL


Last update: 14 April, 2020

An open academic seminar series for everyone interested in water economics.

To receive information about our seminars (including Zoom URLs), sign up by registering through this form. In case you are willing to present at a SWELL seminar, please contact Erik Ansink. Submissions (long abstracts or full papers) will be accepted on a rolling basis and are considered by a small editorial team. Presentations (live, on Zoom) follow a standard academic 15-minute format + 5 minutes online discussion. Schedule dates and times are discussed with presenters and chosen such that they facilitate participation from around the world.
